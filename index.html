About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to partner internation players in their quest to invest in Zimbabwe in various sectors such as infrastructure development, data science, E-learning and online banking.

Why choose us?

AJICOM WIRELESS PRIVATE LIMITED (“The Company”) is a legally registered company registered and domiciled in Zimbabwe in terms of the Companies Act. The company was registered in 2019 after its directors and key team members regrouped to harness their individual exposures in national and international in various sectors to take lead in the development of the Zimbabwe after more than 30 years of economic decline. The company conducts its business mainly from its head office which is located in Harare, the capital of Zimbabwe and its registered office being 2773 Sunway City, Ruwa.
